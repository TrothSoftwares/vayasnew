About Organic Coffee

Its more Special than You Think.

Each specialty coffee requires specially picked plantations with teams of experts that have complete knowledge of every condition necessary for each of the coffees produced. This is what Ekata’s team executes with precision, planning, and dedication to produce the best specialty coffee consistently so that every single consignment leaves consumers beyond satisfied.

The teams of experts audit complete post-harvest processes from the farm on a consistent basis to improve quality and ensure only the finest specialty coffee is produced under the Ekata brand.

Different Types of Speciality Coffees

Speciality Organic Red Honey Coffees

Red Honey coffees are created with perfect care and nurturing. Red Blood Cherry’s are only used to create these sustainable coffees ( no water is used to avoid polluting the rivers - it’s a big seen ). Honey bees can be found in the process around the coffees. The sweetness of the mucilage slowly moves into coffee and giving sweet coffees. It’s also called PSD pulped Sun-Dried.

Speciality Organic Naturals

Naturals are also a process where no water is involved and the fruitiness from these blood red crimson cherries get transferred to the beans and it’s sweeter than the other coffees, it’s a very delicate process and it needs to be taken care in all levels of processing.

Demeter Red Honey Coffees

Demeter is a Biodynamic coffee it has the same as the above honey collection process. But this is Demeter standards the highest in the organic international standards which German and Japan follows mostly.

Demeter Natural Coffees

Natural are also a process where no water is involved and the fruitiness from these blood red crimson cherries get transferred to the beans and it’s sweeter than the other coffees, it’s a very delicate process and it needs to be taken care in all levels processing. But it’s Demeter Biodynamic coffees.

Indian Costa Rican PSD

Foreign origin coffees grown in Indian Baba Budan land of coffee, this is Costa Rican beans grown in India for 15 years. We are doing honey PSD in this variety.

Speciality Organic Cascara

Cascara is the skin of the coffee, it’s used in Yemen and Ethiopia ever since, now it’s trending in Europe due to its high content of caffeine and antioxidants, this is Perfect for all cold brew and sweet flavored cold drip.

Speciality Organic Yellow Burbons

This is organic washed coffees from this variety of coffee coming from bourbon island Madagascar. The specialty is the variety and organic.

High Elevation - Honey Coffee & Natural Specialty coffees

The highest elevation coffee farm producing the honey and Naturals mentioned above, the specialty is the elevation and Baba Budan Mountains.

Catimore Variety Honey & Washed arabic coffees

Catimore variety honey and naturals coffee from an organic farm.
